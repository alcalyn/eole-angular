Hello, world!

Eole is a board game website. You can play in real-time with whoever you want.

It's free and free software.

{{ 'home.play'|translate }}

I want to play

You can navigate games and create a party or join a party someone has created.

I want to develop a game

You have to develop a PHP Api, and maybe a websocket topic for real-time.
You also have to develop, for the web interface, an AngularJs module to plug your game to Eole.
